1. Getting online


Offline Start Kit to get you started and find your access to our online ressource. All the basics are clearely explained and illustrated. Once on our website, create your account and define a password: you are about to start your journey with us!






2. Design your program


Discover our website and design your personal journey. First define your goals and then have an overview of the website to see an exhaustive list of classes we propose. Prioritise your tasks: you won’t be able to do everything, some classes are more time-consuming than others!






3. Start your program


Start your online program with personalised lessons and interactive trainings. You can go at your own pace and you are not even obliged to finish every class. Feel free to use our platform the way you want, so that you learn what is really interesting for you. On the other hand, feel free to skip classes you are not interested in: our goal is to make sure you can learn to master technology quickly!
